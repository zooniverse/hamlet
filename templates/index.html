Welcome to the Hamlet service. This service allows Zooniverse Project Owners to export their Project data to various external services and make subsequent changes to their Zooniverse datasets based on ML results.

You should see a list of your projects below. Click on the link underneath the project for the service you would like to use. The options are:

  1. AutoML Export – export your data to Google Cloud Storage and train a custom ML model.
  2. Camera Traps Subject Assistant – sort empty/wildlife images from camera traps.
  3. Zoobot Subject Assistant – sort galaxy types.
